About

Sustainable practice at the intersection of art and fashion

XinXinWang is a London-based artist working across painting, mixed media, and fashion-adjacent forms. The practice centres on sustainability—favouring recycled, responsibly sourced, or low-impact materials— and explores how garments and canvases can both carry memory, place, and care.

Current studio research looks at material lifecycles: dyes made from natural or kitchen waste, repurposed textiles, and surfaces that can be repaired rather than discarded. The aim is to keep beauty and rigour without wastefulness—letting craft and ethics sit together.

Based in London, UK. Studying at Kingston School of Art.
